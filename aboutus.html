ABOUT US

Welcome to Paw's Pet Shop and thank you for visiting our website! We appreciate you being curious about our company and look forward to seeing you in our shop. In the meantime, below is a little information about Paw's Pet Shop.

Paw's Pet Shop is a locally owned and operated pet food and supply store. Our mission is to provide dog and cat parents with everything they need to ensure their beloved pets live long and happy lives. We love talking about all types of pets and we're even more excited when you bring your furry family member into the store to meet us! We are constantly researching and learning about new products that can make our pets' lives better.

Our goal is to provide a clean and pet-friendly store that has everything your dog or cat needs. Although our store is not very big, we offer a fast, friendly, local and cheaper experience for you to afford. Located at 377, Jalan Dato Lela Maharaja, Taman Seri Rembau, 71300 Rembau, Negeri Sembilan. We would like to provide services to all Dog and Cat owners in the surrounding area by offering a low cost local alternative to pet stores. We carry several brands of Dog/Cat food, including most major brands. We also have access to many brands that are not on our shelves so, if you don't see what you are looking for, please ask about special orders! We also have a large selection tools that pets requirements:

  • Toys
  • Food
  • Grooming supplies
  • Supplementary food
  • External supplies

    Our doors opened for the first time on December 22, 2021 and we have been growing ever since. Thank you again for visiting our site and learning about our company! We will see you soon.
